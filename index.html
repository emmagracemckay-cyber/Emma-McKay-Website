Welcome!

On this website, you'll find more about me, including my background, resume, and interests. Use the toggle menu to explore and learn more.

Hi everyone! I'm Emma, and I come from the beautiful mountain town of Huntsville, Utah, basically the kind of place that makes you want to go outside and pretend you're in a movie montage. Growing up as one of nine kids (yes, nine!), I learned early on how to share, collaborate, and hold my own in a debate over who gets the last slice of pizza. It also taught me how to work hard, laugh often, and turn just about anything into an adventure.

I'm currently studying business at Brigham Young University, and I'm especially excited to dive deeper into experience design and management. I've always been fascinated by how businesses can create meaningful, memorable experiences and I'm even more excited about using that to make a positive impact. My long-term goal is to work in sustainability-focused retail, helping create stylish, functional clothing while reducing waste (think fashion that's as kind to the planet as it is cute). I'm also planning to pursue a Master's in Public Administration and eventually work in education reform, because I care deeply about making systems better for people.

Entrepreneurship has been part of my journey for a while. I started my own shaved ice business in high school, which taught me everything from customer service to problem-solving (and how to handle a line of impatient people on a hot day). Right now, I'm also working toward a global business degree and have a minor in global and community impact, because I want my work to matter beyond just the bottom line.

Outside of school, I'm happiest when I'm creating or exploring. I love reading (seriously, I will always recommend a good book), getting lost in K-dramas, sewing my own clothes, and experimenting in the kitchen, especially with foods from different cultures. You'll also find me hiking, swimming, painting, crafting, or just being outdoors whenever I can.

Above all, I value building meaningful connections and learning from others’ experiences. I’m excited to continue growing, collaborating, and creating a positive impact.
